At ABES Engineering College, we ignite the flames of curiosity,
knowledge, and innovation. Established over two decades ago
under the esteemed Society for Educational Excellence, our
institution has evolved into a dynamic center of learning nestled in
the heart of Ghaziabad. Our unwavering commitment to excellence has
garnered us noteworthy positions in the National Institute Ranking
Framework (NIRF), a testament to our dedication to quality education.

Beyond engineering, our academic spectrum encompasses a diverse range
of programs, including M.Tech, MCA, and MBA courses, designed to cater
to the multifaceted aspirations of our students. As we stride forward,
our core values of integrity, excellence, and innovation remain at our
foundation. ABES Engineering College is not just an institution;
it's a journey of transformation, empowerment, and enlightenment.
Join us on this extraordinary voyage, where potential finds its path, and
dreams take flight.
